Bagakay is a barangay in the municipality of Dapa, in the province of Surigao del Norte. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 550. This represented 1.90% of the total population of Dapa. The population of Bagakay grew from 316 in 1990 to 550 in 2020, an increase of 234 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 3.13%, or an increase of 75 people, from the previous population of 475 in 2015.
